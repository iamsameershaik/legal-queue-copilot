PARTNERSHIP NON-DISCLOSURE AND COLLABORATION AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into between:

MediaGroup plc, a company incorporated in England and Wales (company number 04512789), with registered office at 88 Broadcast Lane, London, W1A 1AA ("MediaGroup"); and

ACME Limited, a company incorporated in England and Wales ("ACME").

1. PURPOSE
The parties wish to explore a potential co-marketing and content partnership. In connection with that exploration, each party may disclose confidential information to the other. This Agreement governs the use of that information.

2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
Confidential Information means any non-public information disclosed by either party, whether in writing, orally, or by any other means, that is designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS
Each party shall hold the other's Confidential Information in confidence and shall not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

4. PUBLICITY AND BRAND RIGHTS
MediaGroup shall have the right to: (a) identify ACME as a MediaGroup partner in MediaGroup's marketing materials, pitch decks, and website; (b) use ACME's name, logo, and trade marks in any MediaGroup publication, press release, or investor communication; and (c) issue press releases announcing the partnership without prior approval from ACME. ACME acknowledges that MediaGroup operates in a time-sensitive media environment and that advance approval is not always practicable.

5. ASSIGNMENT
MediaGroup may assign this Agreement and all rights hereunder to any MediaGroup group company or to a purchaser of the MediaGroup business without notice to or consent from ACME.

6. TERM
This Agreement shall be in effect for one year from the date of signing. Upon expiry, all confidentiality obligations shall cease. The parties agree there is no need for obligations to survive beyond the contract period.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
