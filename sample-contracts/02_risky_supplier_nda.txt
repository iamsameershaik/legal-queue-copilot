NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is made as of this day between:

TechCorp Inc., a Delaware corporation with offices at 100 Market Street, San Francisco, California ("TechCorp"); and

ACME Limited ("Recipient").

1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
TechCorp may disclose Confidential Information to Recipient. Recipient agrees to keep all such information strictly confidential.

2. OBLIGATIONS
Recipient shall: hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence; not disclose to third parties; use only for purposes of evaluating a potential business relationship.

3. INDEMNIFICATION
Recipient shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless TechCorp, its officers, directors, employees, agents, and successors from and against any and all claims, damages, losses, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or related to Recipient's breach of this Agreement or Recipient's use of TechCorp's Confidential Information. This indemnification obligation is unlimited and shall survive termination of this Agreement indefinitely.

4. LIABILITY
NOTWITHSTANDING ANY OTHER PROVISION OF THIS AGREEMENT, IN NO EVENT SHALL TECHCORP BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES. RECIPIENT'S LIABILITY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT SHALL BE UNLIMITED.

5. PUBLICITY
Recipient grants TechCorp the right to identify Recipient as a partner or customer and to use Recipient's name, logo, and trade marks in TechCorp's marketing materials, website, press releases, and investor presentations without further consent.

6. AUTO-RENEWAL
This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods unless terminated by either party upon 14 days' written notice prior to the end of the then-current term.

7. ASSIGNMENT
TechCorp may assign this Agreement and its rights hereunder to any third party without consent or notice.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California, without regard to conflict of law principles.

9. SURVIVAL
Recipient's confidentiality obligations shall survive indefinitely.

Signed:
TechCorp Inc: ______________________
ACME Limited: ______________________
