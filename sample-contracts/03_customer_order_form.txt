CUSTOMER ORDER FORM AND TERMS OF SERVICE

Order Reference: SOF-2024-00891
Date: As of order submission

SUPPLIER: DataSoft Solutions Ltd, 200 Tech Park, Manchester, M1 7PF ("Supplier")
CUSTOMER: ACME Limited, 1 King Street, London, EC2V 8AD ("Customer")

SERVICES ORDERED:
- DataSoft Analytics Platform (Enterprise Tier)
- Onboarding and implementation support
- Annual subscription

COMMERCIAL TERMS:
Annual subscription fee: £48,000 per annum
Payment terms: Net 90 days from invoice date

AUTO-RENEWAL:
This Order Form and the subscription shall automatically renew for successive 12-month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 15 days prior to the end of the then-current subscription term.

SECURITY AUDIT RIGHTS:
Customer agrees to grant Supplier the right to conduct periodic security assessments and audits of Customer's IT systems and infrastructure to verify compliance with Supplier's security standards. Such audits may be conducted at any time with 24 hours' notice.

DATA PROCESSING:
Customer grants Supplier a broad licence to collect, process, analyse, and store all data input into or generated by the platform, including for purposes of product improvement, benchmarking, and anonymised analytics. Supplier may share aggregated data with third parties.

INDEMNITY:
Customer shall indemnify Supplier against all losses, damages, claims and expenses arising from Customer's use of the platform, including claims by Customer's own end users.

LIABILITY:
Supplier's total liability shall not exceed £500. Customer's liability is unlimited.

GOVERNING LAW:
This agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
