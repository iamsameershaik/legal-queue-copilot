ENTERPRISE MASTER SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT — ORDER FORM

Order Reference: EMSA-2024-04471
Effective Date: As of execution

VENDOR: CloudBase Corporation, a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 500 Enterprise Parkway, Austin, Texas 78701 ("CloudBase")
CUSTOMER: ACME Limited, a company incorporated in England and Wales ("Customer")

SERVICES: CloudBase Enterprise Infrastructure Platform — Tier 1 contract
Annual contract value: £240,000
Contract term: 36 months from effective date

PAYMENT TERMS:
All amounts are due and payable net ninety (90) days from invoice date. CloudBase reserves the right to charge interest on overdue amounts at the rate of 18% per annum.

LIABILITY:
CloudBase's aggregate liability under this agreement shall not exceed one hundred pounds sterling (£100). Customer's liability under this agreement is unlimited and shall survive termination of the agreement indefinitely.

INDEMNIFICATION:
Customer shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless CloudBase, its affiliates, officers, directors, agents, employees, and successors from and against any and all claims, losses, damages, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or relating to: (a) Customer's use of the platform; (b) any breach by Customer; (c) claims by Customer's end users; and (d) any claims by third parties arising from data processed through the platform. This indemnification is unlimited and irrevocable.

DATA PROCESSING:
Customer grants CloudBase a perpetual, worldwide, irrevocable licence to collect, process, analyse, retain, and commercialise all data generated by Customer's use of the platform, including for CloudBase's product development, benchmarking, machine learning training, and third-party analytics partnerships.

AUTO-RENEWAL:
This agreement shall automatically renew for successive 36-month terms unless either party provides written notice at least 7 days prior to the end of the then-current term.

SECURITY AUDIT RIGHTS:
Customer grants CloudBase and its designated third parties the right to conduct security audits of Customer's IT infrastructure, internal systems, and employee devices at any time with 2 hours' notice. Customer shall provide full access including to systems not directly related to the CloudBase platform.

GOVERNING LAW:
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas.
